About Eco Earn

More about Eco Earn

Our Aim

Our innovative plastic recycling platform offers a convenient solution for eco-conscious individuals. Simply upload a photo of your plastic items, and we'll connect you with the nearest retailer or recycling center. Companies and organizations can offer points based on the quantity and quality of plastic you recycle. These points can be redeemed for fantastic discounts and coupons on their party websites.

Our Mission

At Eco Earn, we're committed to making recycling accessible, rewarding, and impactful. Our mission is to reduce plastic waste while creating economic incentives that make sustainable choices the easy choice.

Our Impact

Since our founding, our community has recycled over 50,000 tons of plastic, preventing it from entering oceans and landfills. Join us in making a positive impact on the environment while enjoying rewards for your sustainable choices.
