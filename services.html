Frequently Asked Questions

How does the points system work?
Our points system awards you based on the weight and type of plastic you recycle. Generally, you earn 10 points per kilogram of recyclable plastic. Different plastic types may earn bonus points based on their recyclability. Points can be tracked in your account and redeemed for various rewards.
What types of plastic do you accept?
We accept all seven common types of plastic (marked with recycling codes 1-7). This includes PET, HDPE, PVC, LDPE, PP, PS, and other plastic types. Our scanning feature can help you identify which category your plastic falls into.
How often can I schedule pickups?
You can schedule pickups weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly depending on your needs. For larger volumes or special circumstances, custom pickup schedules can be arranged through our customer service.
What rewards can I redeem my points for?
Points can be redeemed for cash payments, gift cards to popular retailers, discounts at our partner businesses, eco-friendly products, or donations to environmental organizations. The rewards catalog is regularly updated in our app.
